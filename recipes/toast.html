Toast Recipe

Description

The simplest thing for you to cook. If you have trouble doing this, you should try again because everybody starts somewhere, and you shouldn't give up! Can be used in many other recipes (such as our own PB&J sandwich recipe!) as a substitute for untoasted bread. Also goes well with a topping such as butter, and a cup of tea.

This recipe assumes the use of a classic top load toaster. Other appliances may be used, however the steps may need to be modified to accommodate.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1 pad of butter

Steps

  1. Prepare your workspace & ingredients.
  2. Remove one slice of bread from the breadbag
  3. Place bread in top of toaster oven
  4. Adjust knob to desired "doneness" of toast
  5. Push down on lever to lower bread slice
  6. Remove the toasted slice when toaster pops up
  7. Place on plate
  8. (Optional) Spread 1 pad of butter on piece of toast
  9. Enjoy!
