Peanut Butter & Jelly Recipe

Description

The classic, the go-to, the absolutely easiest thing to assemble when you just want something sweet and a little filling. The PB&J sandwich is a mainstay for a reason, and holds a special place in most of our childhoods. Today I will break it down for you so you can read how to make one if you ever forget.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread (preferably something like white or whole grain)
  • Jar of peanut butter
  • Jar of jelly, jam, or preserves

Steps

  1. Prepare your workspace & ingredients.
  2. Pull out two slices of bread
  3. On one slice of bread spread a layer of peanut butter
  4. On the other slice of bread spread a layer of jelly
  5. Align the two slices and put them together, ingredients sides in
  6. Cut diagonally or vertically depending on preference
  7. CONSUME
